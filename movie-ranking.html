My Top 3 Movies of all times

Inside Out 2

Pixar's sequel cleverly explores teenage emotions with heart, humor, and stunning animation—earning praise for its emotional depth and relatability.

Deadpool & Wolverine

This R‑rated Marvel crossover smashed box office norms, becoming the highest‑grossing R‑rated film ever and a fan favorite. It delivers a lively mix of action, irreverent humor, and nostalgia with Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman .

Despicable Me 4

This fourth installment in the Despicable Me franchise continued to charm with its lovable characters—Minions antics, heartfelt storytelling, and universal family appeal.
